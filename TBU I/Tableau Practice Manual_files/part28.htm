Practice: Creating Groups and Hierarchies

Explore both groups and hierarchies.


You currently have a view that displays average price of wine bottles for the United States broken down by State. Notice that there is a State called Washington-Oregon as well as separate entries for Washington and Oregon. Create a group to combine all Washington and Oregon entries together. Then create a hierarchy to drill up and down between the group and the states.
