View One


Currently you have average shipping costs by cities in the U.S. market. You would like to analyze your data aggregated by state. Use split to create a new field for state, so you can easily determine where your shipping costs are highest.


DIRECTIONS

1. Open Using Splits Starter.twbx

2. On the Data Source page, use Split to separate the City, State field into two fields.

1. Rename the new fields “City” and “State”.

2. On the Average Shipping Costs worksheet, drag the State field to Rows on top of the City, State field to replace it.


SELF CHECK: Which state has the highest average shipping cost?
