Build A Dashboard


1. Click the New Dashboard tab to add a dashboard.


2. Under Sheets, drag the worksheet US Medals by Sport to the dashboard on Drop sheets here.

3. Drag the worksheet Medal Breakdown by Sport to the bottom half of the dashboard and drop when you see the gray box.



4. Drag the worksheet Medals by Gender to the right of Medal Breakdown by Sport on the dashboard.
